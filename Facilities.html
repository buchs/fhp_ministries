Facilities Being Served

Faith, Hope and Peace Ministries is now serving the following 19 facilities in the Chicago-land area. (Attendance for the week ending June 29, 2018 follows in parentheses)

  • Atria, (formerly Sunrise Senior Living) Glen Ellyn, IL (16)
  • Alden Debes, Rockford, IL (18)
  • Alden Estates, Orland Park, IL (8)
  • Bria, Westmont, IL (15)
  • Briar Place, Indian Head Park, IL (26)
  • Brighton Gardens, Wheaton, IL (13)
  • Brookdale, Burr Ridge, IL (23)
  • Caledonia Home, North Riverside, IL (12)
  • Cantata (British Home), Brookfield, IL (13)
  • Clarendale, Algonquin, IL (12)
  • Cordia Senior Residence, Westmont, IL (11)
  • Deer Path Senior Living, Huntley, IL (11)
  • EastGate Manor, Algonquin, IL (9)
  • GlenBridge Nursing and Rehab Center, Niles, IL (26)
  • Lombard Place, Lombard, IL (18)
  • Mayslake Village, Oak Brook, IL (7)
  • Scottish Home, North Riverside, IL (17)
  • Sunrise Senior Living, Lombard, IL (15)
  • Sunrise Senior Living, Naperville, IL (16)

This week more than 286 people worshiped together at these 19 facilities, celebrating and praising our Lord, Jesus Christ.Visitors are always welcome! If you have a loved one in a residential facility that we do not currently serve, please contact us so we might consider providing a service there. We work to bring the Gospel message to all in need. Friends and Family are always welcome!
